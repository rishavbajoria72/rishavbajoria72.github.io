About

We are a packaging company coming up with eco-friendly packaging solutions. Our bags can be resued multiple times & can be recycled after use.

  • Bag Material

    We use 100% cotton fabric

    Our bags are made of pure cotton fabric with no trace of synthetics.

  • Stitching

    A complete bag making unit

    The manufacturing unit has 70 people working round the clock. The fabric is stitched by our skilled workers to give you the desired output.

  • Printing

    Bags undergo Screen Printing

    We value your brand and hence custom screens are made to print your design
